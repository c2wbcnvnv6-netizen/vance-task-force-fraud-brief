How to Submit a FOIA Request

1. Use FOIA.gov (fastest for most agencies)
Go to foia.gov, select the agency, paste the generated text, and submit. You will receive a tracking number by email.
2. Agency-specific portals
GSA: gsa.gov FOIA
HHS: hhs.gov/foia
OMB: Use FOIA.gov or the White House FOIA portal.
3. Keep records
Save the tracking number, date submitted, and a copy of your request. Follow up after 20 business days if you have not received an acknowledgment.
Important Tips
  • • Requests for aggregated statistics and policy documents are far less likely to be denied than requests naming specific vendors under active review.
  • • Always request a fee waiver on public-interest grounds (included by default in the builder).
  • • You can request a rolling production — ask the agency to release records as they become available.
  • • If denied or heavily redacted, you have 90 days to appeal. The template language supports this.
  • • Reference the April 2026 public statements about the White House Anti-Fraud Task Force findings when relevant.
